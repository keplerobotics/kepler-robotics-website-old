Industrial Training Open

April 27 2020

Robots are undoubtedly one of the coolest and the most useful inventions. Most of us usually get motivated to create a robot either by watching these magnificent things in science fiction films or bot-battles in technical festivals. But the making of a homegrown robot needs a solid fundamental understanding of the concepts behind it. This internship will teach you the general processes for building a robot, starting from the abstraction, to having a fully functional robot. This internship is open for everyone, whether you are a beginner or an advanced maker, who wants to understand the complete process of building a Robotic system.

Here are some highlights of our training:

Internship Oppurtunities for best performers

The basics of Electronics

The basics of Robotics

How to Design your Robot step by step

3D design and fabrication of robot parts and its assembly
