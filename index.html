About Us

KEPLER ROBOTICS is formed with the intention of improving the skills and creativity of students. Our workshops training programs help young students become practical, hands-on and creative. We strongly believe that complicated concepts can be taught in a simple manner using innovative methodologies. Through our well-structured lectures, workshops, and other practical sessions, we help students understand the complicated concepts of Robotics and design in an interesting way. Our sessions are planned to help students open up their mind and begin to look at engineering in a much broader perspective.

Our training modules give utmost importance to design and individual thinking. All our programs would involve brainstorming design sessions to give the students an opportunity to come up with their own solutions. Our kits have minimal pre-designed parts encouraging the students to apply their own designs while fabricating their robots.

What We Do

Robotics

The Robotics wing of KEPLER associates with many higher secondary schools and colleges to train the students in various fields in Robotics. Training is imparted in association with their curriculum of the campus. Through our training, the students get a clear idea of making simple robots from School itself. The various usage of soft and hard tools helps them to grow their career in an easier way. We open a new window of learning technology in the most adaptive way.

Internship Training

Short term and long term internship to the aspirants, both college-going and degree students are provided. Interns will get an opportunity to get trained in new areas of related fields and exposure to the industrial environment. We mainly focus in a large number of areas including Embedded systems, PCB Design, VLSI, Internet of Things(IoT), Robotics,3D designing, and Fabrication.

Training Programs and workshops

Training programs for students and faculty members are being coordinated regularly. Both short term and long term courses are provided. The training programs are done in our labs or in any technical institution as per the demand. Our qualified faculty will bring the equipment and devices to the campus and undertake the training. Courses on specific topics are arranged both in our institute and outside.

Makerspace and FabLab

KEPLER LABS provides technical facilities including makerspace, technical information, equipment, etc. Guidance is provided to students who do their creative projects. The guidance includes providing the hardware, PCB fabrication, optimization of the circuits, prototype making, etc.
